Sourcing & Technical FAQ

Providing transparent, industrial-grade answers for procurement teams, apparel manufacturers, and global sourcing agencies. Our goal is to streamline your bulk custom patch production lifecycle.

01 / Commercial & Volume Sourcing

Q: What is the factory-direct minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

A: Our B2B production lines are optimized for bulk manufacturing starting at 50 pieces per design. For enterprise-level apparel brands, we provide aggressive tiered pricing for volumes of 1,000, 10,000, and 50,000+ pieces. This scale allows us to provide full technical support—including free digitizing and high-res digital proofing—without the overhead of small-scale retail markups.

Q: Do you offer enterprise supply contracts for seasonal garment replenishment?

A: Yes. We partner with international uniform suppliers and streetwear brands for multi-year supply chains. We store your specific thread-maps, design files, and physical samples in our archive for up to 5 years, ensuring that re-orders placed 12 months apart maintain 100% stitch-density and color consistency.

02 / Technical & Manufacturing standards

Q: How do you guarantee Pantone Solid Coated color matching?

A: We cross-reference your brand's official PMS numbers against our inventory of over 300 industrial-grade polyester yarns. We do not rely on screen-based RGB colors. Every bulk order includes a high-resolution digital embroidery proof that specifies the exact thread codes for your procurement team's approval before we initiate the machines.

Q: Are your patches certified for industrial-laundry and high-heat drying?

A: Absolutely. We utilize high-tenacity, bleach-resistant threads and heavyweight twill bases specifically engineered for the garment industry. Our emblems are tested to withstand commercial wash temperatures up to 90°C and industrial tumble drying without thread pilling, adhesive failure, or border fraying.

Q: What is the maximum size for a custom embroidered back patch?

A: Utilizing our multi-head Tajima machinery, we can manufacture oversized rockers and back emblems up to 15 inches (38cm) in width. For these large format items, we recommend a reinforced double-twill base or a heavyweight felt backing to ensure the patch remains stable and flat on denim or leather outerwear.

03 / Compliance, Logistics & Sustainability

Q: Do your raw materials meet OEKO-TEX Standard 100 requirements?

A: Yes. All polyester yarns, cotton bases, and thermal adhesives used in our facility pass strict OEKO-TEX Standard 100 Class I certification (suitable for infants). Our products are guaranteed to be free from harmful chemicals, lead, and phthalates, making them safe for global retail markets, including those with strict CPSIA requirements.

Q: How do you manage global express and sea-freight logistics?

A: We are a specialized B2B export facility. For medium-volume orders, we utilize 3-5 day air express (FedEx/DHL/UPS) with specialized duty-inclusive (DDP) options for many countries. For massive container-level orders, we coordinate with your global freight forwarders for FOB or CIF sea transport directly from our hub.
