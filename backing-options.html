Technical Backing Guide

Selecting the appropriate backing is fundamental to the long-term structural integrity and functional application of a custom emblem. Our 23 years of manufacturing expertise ensures that your brand identity remains permanently bonded to your garment or equipment.

Engineering the Perfect Attachment

For B2B procurement teams and apparel manufacturers, the choice of patch backing is not merely an aesthetic decision—it is a chemical and structural requirement. The backing must match the base fabric's composition, the anticipated laundry cycle frequency, and the specific functional requirements of the end-user. Whether you are producing military uniforms that require modularity or high-end fashion garments that require a soft, natural drape, Chamguan Patch provides specialized industrial mounting solutions.

Our facility utilizes computerized hot-press equipment and high-tenacity industrial threads to apply our backings. We audit every batch of adhesive to ensure constant melt-points and superior peel-strength. From traditional sew-on methods to modern magnetic and specialized heat-seals, we provide 8 distinct B2B options tailored for clothing brands, promotional agencies, and tactical equipment designers worldwide.

Hook & Loop (Industrial Grade)

Commonly known by the brand name Velcro, this backing features high-density nylon hooks and soft loops designed for over 5,000 detach cycles. We perimeter-stitch the Hook & Loop directly to the emblem base to prevent delamination. **Best For:** Tactical vests, modular backpacks, and military uniforms.

Heat-Seal Iron-On

Coated with a high-melt-point polyethylene adhesive. Designed for professional apparel factory heat-press machines (150°C for 15 seconds). Our glue formula is specifically engineered to be laundry-safe and dry-clean compatible. **Best For:** Denim, heavy cotton hoodies, and beanies.

Adhesive (Peel & Stick)

A pressure-sensitive temporary backing with a paper release liner. Ideal for short-term branding at corporate events, trade shows, and conferences. Note: This is not a permanent solution for washed garments. **Best For:** Promotional events and temporary gift branding.

Plain / Sew-On

The traditional factory choice. Features no adhesive layer, preserving the patch's natural flexibility and the garment's organic drape. Must be stitched permanently using a single-needle or Merrow machine. **Best For:** High-end fashion, light knitwear, and long-term uniform issues.

Wholesale Backing FAQ

Q: Can Iron-On patches be applied to polyester or synthetic fabrics?

A: Yes, but with caution. High-heat glues require 150°C, which can scorch certain synthetics. For sportswear brands, we recommend our specialized Low-Melt adhesive or traditional sewing to maintain the fabric's integrity.

Q: How do you prevent Hook & Loop patches from peeling at the corners?

A: We solve this by using "Perimeter Reinforcement." Rather than just gluing the fastener to the patch, our factory sew-teams stitch the Hook & Loop around the entire boundary using high-tenacity thread, ensuring the edges remain flush under thousands of detachments.

Q: Do you offer backings that are safe for infants' and children's clothing?

A: Absolutely. All our adhesives and backings are certified under OEKO-TEX Standard 100 Class I (safe for infants). They are free from phthalates, formaldehydes, and heavy metals, meeting all international B2B compliance requirements.
