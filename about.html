Frequently Asked Factory Questions

Q: Can you match exact PMS (Pantone) colors for our apparel brand?

A: Yes. We stock over 300 thread shades matching the international Pantone Matching System (PMS). Our digitizers will specify thread numbers on your digital pre-production proof before machine spin-up.

Q: What is your actual lead time for massive bulk orders of 10,000+ pieces?

A: Thanks to our 50+ Tajima embroidery heads, we have high-volume capabilities. A bulk order of 10,000 pieces typically takes 10 to 14 working days. We also support staggered container shipping for global logistics pipelines.

Q: Do your embroidery threads withstand hot industrial washes?

A: Absolutely. We use 100% premium polyester threads that are engineered for heavy wear and tear. They are bleedproof, fade-resistant, and withstand industrial washing processes and hot tumble drying without shrinking or losing color.
