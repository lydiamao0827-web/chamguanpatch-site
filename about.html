Wholesale Manufacturing FAQ

Q: How does Chamguan ensure color consistency for large clothing brands?

A: We cross-reference every design against the Pantone Solid Coated (PMS) system. Our warehouse stocks over 300 thread shades, and our sales engineers provide digital thread-map proofs for approval before mass production begins.

Q: What are your factory's standard lead times for bulk export orders?

A: For standard bulk orders of 1,000 to 5,000 pieces, our factory fulfillment time is 7-10 working days. For larger container-level orders, we provide a staggered production schedule to match your brand's logistics pipeline.

Q: Can you provide customized export packing and labeling for our warehouse?

A: Yes. We support custom SKU labeling, barcodes, and specialized inner-bag counts to ensure seamless integration into your brand's distribution center upon arrival.
