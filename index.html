B2B Edge & Mounting Specifications

Choose from multiple mounting backings and edge border cuts designed specifically for uniform attachment, retail apparel sewing, or tactical gear swapping.

Heavy-Duty Mounting Backings

01
Iron-on Backing

Coated with a highly durable heat-activated adhesive. Best for fashion brands, hoodies, and jackets. Highly resistant to commercial dry cleaning.

02
Sew-on Backing (Plain)

Traditional option without adhesive or backing material. Offers maximum flexibility, natural drape, and is perfect for luxury garments and sewing directly onto caps.

03
Hook & Loop Backing

Features industrial-grade Hook & Loop fasteners. Highly durable, engineered for tactical gear, police/military uniforms, and modular apparel systems.

04
Adhesive Backing (Peel & Stick)

Temporary sticky backing designed for promotional corporate events, conventions, and short-term applications. Simple application to standard flat surfaces.

Border Edge Cuts

01
Merrow Border

The classic wrap-around stitched edge. Highly durable and traditional, it is perfect for symmetrical shapes like circles, shields, and rectangles.

02
Laser Cut Border

Utilizes highly precise CO2 laser cutters to burn edges on intricate, irregular custom shapes. Guarantees clean borders that will never fray or unravel.

03
Stitched Border (Flat Stitch)

Features flat, dense satin stitching along the boundary. Highly minimalist and modern, designed for seamless integration on retail brand garments.

Sourcing Support

Wholesale Procurement FAQ

Review critical manufacturing specifications, technical limits, and commercial wholesale parameters below.

Q1: What are the minimum order quantities for bulk runs?

Our standard production minimum is 50 pieces per design. This enables us to properly calibrate our industrial multi-head embroidery machines, allocate high-end materials, and provide free artwork review without setup fees.

Q2: How do you ensure correct brand color matching?

We match Pantone Solid Coated (PMS) reference numbers to premium embroidery thread palettes. We submit digital thread alignment proofs and physical pre-production stitch outs for approval before mass runs.

Q3: What are the specific technical file requirements?

We highly recommend vector files saved as Adobe Illustrator (.AI), PDF, EPS, or SVG formats. High-resolution raster images (.PNG or .JPG, minimum 300 DPI) are also accepted and processed by our design department.

Q4: What is the typical turn-around time for wholesale orders?

Our general turnaround time is 7 to 10 days for manufacturing, following final sample approval. Rush order options are available for specialized uniform contract deadlines or launch windows.

Q5: Do you supply Hook & Loop and adhesive backing styles?

Yes. We supply robust Hook & Loop, heat-activated Iron-on, temporary sticky Adhesive, standard plastic backing, safety pin backings, and plain borders for direct sew-on apparel integration.

Q6: Are your materials safe for commercial children's clothing?

Absolutely. All polyester thread, twill, and backing chemicals used in our factory pass strict OEKO-TEX Standard 100 tests, guaranteeing they are skin-safe and chemical-free.
