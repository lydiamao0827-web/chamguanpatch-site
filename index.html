Wholesale Patch Manufacturing FAQ

Q: What is the absolute minimum for a factory-direct bulk order?

A: At Chamguan Patch, our production facility is optimized for wholesale volume. We accept bulk orders starting at 50 pieces per design. This scale allows us to provide professional digitizing and Tajima machine programming without charging hidden setup fees, ensuring a high-end product at true factory pricing.

Q: How do you handle Pantone (PMS) color matching for global clothing brands?

A: We maintain an inventory of over 300 polyester and rayon thread shades that are mapped to the Pantone Solid Coated reference system. Before production begins, our engineering team provides high-resolution digital proofs specifying the thread codes, ensuring your brand identity remains 100% consistent across years of repeat orders.

Q: Are your raw materials compliant with international safety standards?

A: Yes. All polyester yarns, cotton-twill fabrics, and high-heat adhesives used in our facility pass strict OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certification. Our products are free from harmful chemicals, safe for prolonged skin contact, and fully suitable for infants' and children's apparel lines.

Q: What is the typical lead time for a bulk export shipment of 10,000 patches?

A: Thanks to our 50+ Japan-imported Tajima machines, we provide rapid turnaround for large-scale procurement. An order of 10,000 pieces typically completes factory fulfillment within 10 to 14 working days. We specialize in coordinated logistics via air or sea freight to match your brand's distribution center deadlines.
