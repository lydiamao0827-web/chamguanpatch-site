Wholesale Patch Sourcing FAQ

Do you work with brands and wholesalers?

Yes. Chamguan Patch is a dedicated B2B manufacturer for bulk custom patches. We partner with international apparel brands, global wholesalers, and sourcing agencies requiring industrial-grade reliability and mass-manufacturing capacity.

What information do you need for a quote?

For an accurate technical quote, we require your logo artwork (AI, PDF, or high-res JPG), specific dimensions, technique type (like custom woven patches or embroidery), backing preference, and total order volume.

Can I upload my artwork directly?

Yes. Use our 'Upload Your Artwork' portal on the 'Get A Quote' page or send files via WhatsApp. Our digitizing team provides a free technical audit to ensure your custom patches are production-ready.

Can you make samples before bulk production?

Absolutely. For every bulk B2B project, we provide a physical pre-production sample for your sign-off. This guarantees the thread colors, texture, and borders meet your exact brand standards before machines start the full run.

What backing and border options do you offer?

We provide industrial-strength Hook and Loop, high-heat adhesives, adhesive peels, and plain sew-on backings. Borders include traditional wrap-around merrowing and precision laser-cut flat edges for modern clothing designs.

How can I contact you quickly for sourcing?

Click the 'Chat on WhatsApp' button for real-time consultation with our sales engineering team. We typically provide technical replies and volume pricing within 12 hours for global sourcing teams.
