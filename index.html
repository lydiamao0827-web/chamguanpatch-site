Wholesale Patch Sourcing FAQ

Q: What is the minimum quantity for a wholesale custom patch order?

A: Our factory is optimized for bulk production. Our standard minimum starts at 50 pieces per design, allowing us to absorb the technical setup costs for high-density machines while providing you with true factory-direct pricing.

Q: How do you handle exact thread color matching for apparel brands?

A: We cross-reference your brand's Pantone (PMS) numbers with our library of 300+ industrial threads. Digital proofs with thread codes are provided for sign-off before machines are programmed.

Q: Are your raw materials compliant with international safety standards?

A: Yes. All polyester yarns, fabrics, and adhesives used in our facility pass strict OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certification. Our products are free from harmful chemicals and safe for skin contact, suitable for infants' and children's apparel.
