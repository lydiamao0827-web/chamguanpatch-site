Thread Density & Coverage Standards

We offer three standard levels of embroidery coverage to match your brand's aesthetic and budget requirements:
• 50% Coverage: Ideal for designs where the background twill fabric is part of the visual aesthetic. Commonly used for simple text or logos on hat patches.
• 75% Coverage: A balance between detail and cost, where most of the surface is embroidered but the twill is used as a background contrast.
• 100% Full Coverage: The most premium option where the entire surface is needle-stitched, providing a heavy, luxurious 3D feel and maximum durability.

Wholesale Sourcing FAQ

Q: What is the factory minimum for custom embroidered patches?

A: Our standard production run starts at 50 pieces per design. For larger brands, we offer aggressive tiered pricing for bulk orders exceeding 1,000, 5,000, and 10,000 units. Bulk manufacturing allows us to absorb setup costs and provide higher stitch density at a lower unit price.

Q: Can you match our specific apparel brand Pantone (PMS) colors?

A: Absolutely. Our design lab maintains a database of over 300 polyester and rayon thread shades mapped directly to the Pantone Solid Coated system. We provide high-resolution digital embroidery proofs with thread codes for brand verification before machines are programmed.

Q: Are the Hook & Loop and Iron-on backings industrial grade?

A: Yes. We use heavy-duty industrial Hook & Loop (often referred to as Velcro-style) that withstands thousands of peel cycles. Our Iron-on backing features a high-temperature melt-point adhesive specifically designed for clothing factory heat-press applications, ensuring a permanent bond that survives industrial laundry.

Q: How do you handle international bulk shipping?

A: We are specialized in global B2B logistics. For small-to-medium bulk orders, we utilize express air freight (FedEx/DHL/UPS) for 3-5 day global delivery. For massive container-level orders, we coordinate with your freight forwarders for FOB/CIF sea or rail transport from our manufacturing hub.
