HD Damask Weaving & Technical Precision

When the visual integrity of a brand logo is too complex for needle-and-thread embroidery, custom woven patches provide the necessary industrial solution. By interweaving thinner, high-tenacity polyester yarns together rather than stitching onto a base fabric, we create a flat, smooth, and incredibly detailed emblem. This process is the gold standard for global apparel brands whose designs contain small text (down to 1.5mm) or geometric patterns that would appear distorted with traditional methods.

At Chamguan Patch, our computerized damask looms are calibrated for high-volume B2B orders. For procurement teams, this ensures 100% graphic fidelity across batches of 100 or 100,000 pieces. Our woven patches are highly resistant to abrasion and pilling, making them ideal for high-friction placements such as elbows, bag exteriors, and internal neck labels. We utilize 100% colorfast yarns that withstand high-temperature industrial laundry cycles without shrinking or losing vibrancy.

Material Integrity & Custom Mounting

We strictly control every stage of the manufacturing lifecycle to ensure your bulk orders exceed international safety standards:
• 50 Denier Yarns: We use ultra-thin 50D threads to achieve the highest resolution possible in a textile product.

Wholesale Woven Sourcing FAQ

Q: Why choose woven over embroidery for our brand's bulk order?

A: Woven patches utilize thinner threads and an interweaving technique that achieves much higher resolution. If your logo has small text, thin lines, or detailed gradients, woven is the superior industrial choice for a sharp, retail-ready result.

Q: Can you provide Hook & Loop (Velcro-style) backing on woven emblems?

A: Absolutely. While woven patches are thinner than embroidered ones, we reinforce them with a high-density backing before mounting the industrial-strength Hook & Loop fasteners. This ensures a rigid, durable product that survives thousands of detach cycles.

Q: How do you handle Pantone (PMS) yarn color matching?

A: We cross-reference your brand's official PMS colors against our inventory of over 200 colorfast yarn shades. We provide digital proofs and physically verified thread maps to ensure global brand consistency across all bulk production batches.

Q: Do you offer bulk discounts for orders over 10,000 pieces?

A: Yes. As a direct factory, we offer aggressive tiered pricing for large-scale procurement. Our sales engineers will provide a comprehensive enterprise pricing table based on your specific volume and frequency requirements.
