Industrial Collegiate Heritage & Modern Streetwear

Chenille patches, traditionally synonymous with American school varsity jackets and athletic awards, have transitioned into a critical branding tool for modern high-fashion and streetwear brands. At Chamguan Patch, we combine 23 years of B2B manufacturing heritage with modern high-speed looping machinery to deliver a product that is both nostalgic and durable. Our chenille emblems are characterized by their consistent loop height, vibrant color saturation, and exceptionally soft, premium hand-feel.

For clothing manufacturers and large-scale wholesalers, we offer "Mixed Media" manufacturing where plush chenille is combined with high-density traditional embroidery. This industrial approach allows the chenille loops to represent the primary design elements, while embroidery captures sharp text, fine outlines, and intricate borders—resulting in a multi-textured product that significantly increases the perceived value of the final garment.

Yarn Loft Integrity & Sourcing Reliability

We audit every batch of raw materials to ensure your bulk orders exceed retail quality standards:

Wholesale Chenille Sourcing FAQ

Q: What is the typical MOQ for custom chenille patch production?

A: Our standard production starts at 50 pieces per design. This volume ensures we can properly calibrate our high-loft yarn spools and maintain consistent 3D texture depth across the entire bulk run, while providing aggressive tiered pricing for large-scale orders.

Q: Can you manufacture oversized back patches for jackets?

A: Yes. We specialize in manufacturing large-format chenille patches up to 15 inches. We use heavyweight double-felt backings and reinforced Merrowed borders to ensure the patch remains stable and flat on the garment during use and shipping.

Q: How do you handle small text or fine details in a chenille design?

A: For small typography or intricate outlines, we utilize a "Mixed Media" approach. The larger, plush elements are produced in chenille, while fine details are executed with high-density flat embroidery for perfect legibility and retail-ready finish.

Q: Is your Hook & Loop and Iron-On backing laundry safe?

A: Yes. We use industrial-grade adhesives specifically formulated for garment factory heat-press applications. Our Hook & Loop backings are sewn with reinforced borders to prevent separation from the felt base through thousands of cycles.
