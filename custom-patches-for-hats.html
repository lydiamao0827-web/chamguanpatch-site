Engineered for Headwear

Manufacturing patches for headwear requires a deep understanding of fabric tension and curved surface application. Unlike standard jacket patches, hat emblems must be flexible enough to conform to the crown of a cap while maintaining a rigid, professional look. At Chamguan Patch, we utilize specialized thin-profile backings and high-density poly-cotton twill to ensure your brand's logo remains crisp and distortion-free after being heat-pressed or sewn onto your headwear line.

Headwear Decoration Standards

We provide a variety of techniques specifically for cap manufacturers:
• 3D Puffed Embroidery: Adds significant dimension and "Pop" to your hat. Ideal for bold, simple logos on snapbacks and dad hats.
• Sublimated Edge Woven: Captures photographic detail while maintaining a thin profile, perfect for beanies and unconstructed caps.
• Embroidered Appliques: Traditional high-thread-count emblems with reinforced Merrowed edges for that classic "Sports Merch" look.

Our 23 years of B2B experience in the headwear sector has allowed us to develop adhesives that withstand the extreme temperatures of industrial heat-presses without damaging the base cap fabric. We provide technical consultation for every order to ensure the mounting method matches your specific headwear material—whether it's heavy acrylic, organic cotton, or technical performance blends.
