Engineered for the Fashion Industry

Manufacturing patches for clothing brands requires a sophisticated understanding of textile chemistry and garment construction. Unlike promotional items, apparel patches must maintain their structural integrity and color vibrancy through repeated home and industrial wash cycles. At Chamguan Patch, we utilize high-tenacity polyester threads and bleedproof dyes to ensure your brand's emblem never compromises the quality of the final garment.
